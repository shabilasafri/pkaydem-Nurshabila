MAIN PROGRAM OF THE ASSOCIATION TO BE CARRIED OUT

1. Happy Home Maintenance (for caregivers of orphans).

2. Management of small hostels for orphans.

3. School financing such as buses and so on.

4. Operation of business programs.

5. Business financial assistance

Small Orphanage

The hostel aims to make it easier for them to participate in co-curricular activities as well as give them time to complete school work.

Happy House (Guardian of Orphans)

For the guardians of orphans where their houses are dilapidated and need help to repair where the construction work is carried out in gotong-royong by the local community. They will also be given business education that suits their interests.
