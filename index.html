BACKGROUND

PERTUBUHAN KEBAJIKAN ANAK YATIM DARUL EHSAN MALAYSIA

This organization is a non-governmental charity (NGO) whose role is to help the poor and the less fortunate, especially orphans, they are also from poor families to get protection and perfect academic education in living life in the future. To help overcome the problem of poverty that plagues the lives of these groups, this organization has taken steps to establish a 2-storey charity house and a surau named RUMAH AMAL ANAK YATIM PKAYDEM was established in lot 873-A, Jalan Banting Klang, Kampong Sungai Manggis, 42700 Banting, Selangor Darul Ehsan

The house is a shelter for orphans and foster children to live their daily lives more perfectly in addition to getting an academic education and deepen their religious knowledge. All these expenses are fully borne by this organization through the assistance and contributions of certain bodies that are sympathetic and concerned about the fate of these children by contributing regularly.

Pertubuhan Kebajikan Anak Yatim Darul Ehsan Malaysia (PKAYDEM) always offers services to protect the underprivileged community to achieve their dreams and is ready to plan to formulate an agenda in line with the achievements of progress.
